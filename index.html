Types Of Tourism In India

India is famous for all type of tourist destinations in one place. We have Adventure Tourism, Cultural Tourism, Eco-Tourism, Religious Tourism and
Film Tourism.

Adventure Tourism

Adventure Tourism is one among the different types of Tourism. according to the Adventure Travel Trade Association, Adventure Tourism is a tourist activity that includes physical activity, a cultural exchange, or activities in nature. Adventure tourism is about connecting with a new culture or a new landscape and being physically active at the same time.

Cultural Tourism

India has the special place for Cultural Tourism beccause of the ancient civilization, as India has been considered as land of ancient history, heitage and culture. Among the various states for cultural tourism in India, Rajasthan is the most popular. The reason for this is that Rajasthan is famous for its rich cultural heritage. The most famous monument is the Taj Mahal in Agra.

Eco-Tourism

Ecotourism in India is a thriving industry owing to the fact that country has managed to save its resplendent natural beauty. In India we have a biological park, zoological parks, tea plantations, wildlife sanctuaries, high mountains and green jungles.

Religious Tourism

India is a country which has choice of journey spiritual growth and enlightenment. Some of the famous religious cities are Amritsar, Ajmer, Fatehpur Sikri, Goa, Velankanni, Fort Kochi, Srirangam, Tirupati, Rameswaram, Thanjavur, Varanasi and Puri.

Film Tourism

The Indian movies leave a huge impact on the minds of viewers, may it be in the form of techniques used, angles shot or locations. When a viewer gets induced to visit a particular location after seeing it in a movie, it is termed as 'Film Tourism'.
